Qadha

AI Interview & Presentation Coach

Project Overview 2025

Qadha is a bilingual AI-powered coaching platform that helps job seekers and professionals practice interviews and presentations — getting real-time analysis, personalized feedback, and model answers powered by state-of-the-art AI, all in Arabic and English.

The Problem
  • Most people never practice interviews before the real thing — and it shows
  • Professional coaching is expensive and inaccessible to many job seekers
  • Generic feedback tools don't adapt to role, experience, or language
  • No dedicated Arabic-language interview practice platform exists
  • Presentation skills rarely get structured, actionable feedback
The Solution
  • AI generates tailored questions based on role, education & experience level
  • Whisper Large V3 transcribes spoken answers with high accuracy
  • GPT-4o scores performance: communication, confidence, answer quality
  • Ideal model answers generated using STAR method per question
  • Full bilingual support — switch between Arabic and English instantly

How It Works
  • Choose interview or presentation mode
  • Select your role, education & experience level
  • AI generates 5 tailored interview questions
  • Record spoken answers — Whisper transcribes in real-time
  • Receive full performance report with scores & ideal answers
  • Track progress over time in the History page
